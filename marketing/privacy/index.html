Privacy Policy

Operated by Shenzhen Chengjing Technology Co., Ltd. (深圳市诚璟科技有限公司, "we", "us", "SendMast"). Effective date: May 25, 2026.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information in connection with the SendMast platform at app.sendmast.com (the "Service"). This policy applies to two distinct groups of individuals:

  • Customers — businesses and their authorized users who register an account and use the Service.
  • End Recipients — natural persons whose contact data Customers upload to the Service so that they may be sent emails. We process End Recipient data only on behalf of and under the instructions of the Customer (i.e., we act as a "processor" / "entrusted party" under PIPL/GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 From Customers (account holders)

  • Account information: name, work email address, password (stored as a salted hash), company name, role, billing contact.
  • Billing information: order amounts, FX rate, payment status, and the partial transaction reference returned by our payment provider (Shouqianba / Alipay / WeChat Pay). We do not store full bank-card or account numbers on our servers.
  • Usage and log data: IP address, user-agent, login timestamps, campaign actions, and error/crash reports.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: a session cookie for login state, and a small number of strictly-necessary cookies for security (CSRF protection, rate limiting). We do not use third-party advertising cookies on the authenticated Service.

2.2 From Customers about End Recipients (entrusted data)

  • Contact data the Customer uploads: typically name, email address, and any custom fields the Customer chooses to import.
  • Email engagement data: delivery, open, click, bounce, and unsubscribe events generated when emails are sent on the Customer's instruction.

Customers must have a lawful basis (typically opt-in consent) to provide End Recipient data to us. We do not collect End Recipient data directly from End Recipients and we do not use it for our own marketing.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, secure, and improve the Service.
  • To send transactional emails to Customer account holders (account verification, password reset, billing receipts, security and service announcements). These transactional messages are delivered via Mailgun.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.
  • To analyze aggregated, de-identified usage patterns for product improvement.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR / PIPL)

We process Customer personal data on the following bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to deliver the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interest — to secure and improve the Service.
  • Consent — for any optional processing where we ask for it explicitly.
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable law.

5. Sharing & Sub-Processors

We do not sell personal data. We share personal data only with the following categories of recipients:

  • Sub-processors we use to operate the Service:
    • Azure Communication Services — outbound email delivery for Customers.
    • Mailgun (Sinch Email) — transactional email delivery for our own platform notifications to Customers.
    • Cloudflare — CDN, DDoS protection, DNS.
    • Shouqianba (收钱吧) — domestic payment aggregator fronting Alipay and WeChat Pay.
    • Hosting infrastructure operated by us (server providers).
  • Authorities — when required by law, court order, or government request, and to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, in which case we will provide notice and the new entity will be bound by this policy.

Each sub-processor is bound by data-protection terms requiring confidentiality and security commensurate with this policy.

6. International Data Transfer

The Service may store and process data on servers located in mainland China and/or other regions where our sub-processors operate. Where data is transferred across borders, we rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms (e.g., standard contractual clauses, PIPL standard contract, or Customer consent).

7. Data Retention

  • Customer account data: retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterwards to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.
  • End Recipient data uploaded by Customers: retained for as long as the Customer keeps it in the Service. Customers can delete contacts and lists at any time. We delete (or anonymize) residual copies on a rolling basis after account closure.
  • Server logs: typically 30–90 days, longer where required for security or legal reasons.

8. Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures including TLS in transit, encryption-at-rest for sensitive fields, role-based access control, audit logging, and regular dependency patching. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete data in certain circumstances ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Receive a portable copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

End Recipients should direct rights requests to the Customer that uploaded their data; we will assist Customers in fulfilling such requests. Customers may exercise their own rights by emailing hello@sendmast.com.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 16 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via email or in-product notice at least 14 days before they take effect.

1. 引言

本隐私政策说明我们在 SendMast 平台 (app.sendmast.com,以下简称"服务") 中如何收集、使用、共享和保护信息。本政策适用于以下两类主体:

  • 客户:在本服务中注册账户并使用本服务的企业及其授权用户。
  • 最终收件人:其联系方式被客户上传至本服务、以便收到客户邮件的自然人。 我们仅根据客户的指示并代表客户处理最终收件人数据(即在 PIPL/GDPR 项下作为"处理者" /"受托方")。

2. 我们收集的信息

2.1 来自客户(账户持有人)

  • 账户信息:姓名、工作邮箱、密码(仅以加盐哈希形式存储)、公司名称、 职务、账单联系人。
  • 支付信息:订单金额、汇率、支付状态以及支付服务商(收钱吧/支付宝/ 微信支付)返回的部分交易引用号。我们不在服务器上保存完整的银行卡号或账户号。
  • 使用和日志数据:IP 地址、User-Agent、登录时间戳、活动操作、错误/ 崩溃报告。
  • Cookie 和类似技术:用于登录状态的会话 Cookie,以及少量严格必要的 Cookie(CSRF 防护、限流)。在已认证的服务区域,我们不使用第三方广告 Cookie。

2.2 客户上传的最终收件人信息(受托数据)

  • 客户上传的联系数据:通常包括姓名、邮箱及客户自定义字段。
  • 邮件互动数据:依据客户指令发送邮件后产生的送达、打开、点击、退信、 退订事件。

客户必须具备合法依据(通常为明示同意 opt-in)才能向我们提供最终收件人数据。我们不直接 从最终收件人处收集数据,也不将其用于我方自身的营销目的。

3. 我们如何使用信息

  • 提供、运营、保护和改进本服务。
  • 向客户账户持有人发送系统类邮件(账户验证、密码重置、账单收据、安全与服务公告); 上述系统邮件经由 Mailgun 投递。
  • 处理支付并防范欺诈。
  • 履行法律义务并应对合法请求。
  • 对脱敏的聚合数据进行使用分析,以改进产品。

4. 法律依据(GDPR / PIPL)

我们在以下依据上处理客户个人信息:

  • 履行合同:为提供您所注册的服务。
  • 合法利益:用于服务的安全和改进。
  • 同意:在我们就特定可选处理征询您的同意时。
  • 法定义务:为遵守适用法律。

5. 共享与受托方

我们不出售个人信息,仅在以下情形下共享:

  • 受托处理方(用于运营本服务):

    • Mailgun (Sinch Email):向客户投递平台自身的系统类邮件。

    • 收钱吧:聚合支付宝、微信支付的国内支付服务商。

每一受托方均受与本政策保密性和安全水平相符的数据保护条款约束。

本服务可能在中国大陆及/或受托方所在的其他地区的服务器上存储和处理数据。如发生跨境传输, 我们将依据相应合法机制(例如标准合同条款、PIPL 标准合同或客户同意)进行。

7. 数据保留
